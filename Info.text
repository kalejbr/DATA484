About the Project-
    Libraries used: Plotly, Dash, Pandas, Numpy, and Dash Bootstrap Components
    Data used: Derived from Jon Snow's works during the 3rd great Cholera Outbreak
        in London. It includes the locations of casualties from the outbreak, the
        locations of 6 water pumps in the SoHo neighborhood, the Census numbers from
        1851, and information regarding a cholera outbreak in Naples, Italy.
